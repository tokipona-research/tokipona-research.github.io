The 120-Word Challenge

Can learning Toki Pona improve student skills?

Welcome to a Unique Research Project

This independent study explores whether learning toki pona - a minimalist constructed language with only 120-140 words - can enhance skills like:

  • Reading comprehension
  • Pattern recognition
  • Mathematical skills
  • General English skills

Join us in this 4-6 week journey to discover how simplicity in language can create complexity in learning!

Note for participants: This study is open to all middle school students. Participation requires parent/guardian consent.

What is Toki Pona?

toki pona means "good language" or "simple language." It was created by linguist Sonja Lang and focuses on fundamental, universal human experiences. With its tiny vocabulary, it forces creative thinking and clear communication.

Example: "friend" = jan pona (good person)
